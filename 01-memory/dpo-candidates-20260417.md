## Sourcing limits encountered

- LinkedIn hit monthly profile-viewing quota after ~40 profiles
  (2nd-degree search). Additional candidates found via "similar profiles"
  suggestions.
- 2nd-degree common connections are cold (multi-year inactive
  acceptances) — not usable as referrals.
- Certifications in French DPO landscape: AFNOR is the strongest signal
  for freelance positioning; AFCDP membership useful; CIPP/E rare in
  freelance pool (usually salaried).
- No profile holds both AFNOR DPO AND CIPP/E — consistent with the rate
  gap (doubly-certified = 8-12k€/month, out of scope).
